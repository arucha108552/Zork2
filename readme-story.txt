In this game will play as the survivor from atomic bomb termination in the world. You wake up and find out that the city have been destroyed and you find out that you have been infect from the radiation. Therefore you have to find a way to cure your self.
================
The stage will be separated into three levels
1. Find news and information about what will be a way to cure
2. Head to the destination along the way you will find many challage things
3. Reach the place and you have to find a way to get cure
